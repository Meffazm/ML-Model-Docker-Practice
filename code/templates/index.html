Predict the sales price of houses in King County, Seattle

Input text file with one instance per line.

Values should be integer or float and comma-separated. One instance should include:

  • Measurement of house in square foot (integer)
  • Overall grade given to the housing unit, based on King County grading system on a scale of 1 to 11 (1-11)
  • Square footage of house apart from basement (integer)
  • Living room area in 2015 (integer)
  • Number of bathrooms in a bedroom of a house (integer)
  • Whether a house has been viewed or not (binary 1/0)
  • Square footage of the basement of the house (integer)
  • Whether a house has a view to waterfront or not (binary 1/0)
  • The date of building of the house (integer)
  • Latitude of the location of the house (float)
  • Number of bedrooms in a house (float)
  • Longitude of the location of the house (float)
